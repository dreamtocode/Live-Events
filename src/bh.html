This group has been created by the organizers of Behance Portfolio Review Tbilisi, Georgia, with the objective to connect local creatives, encourage exchange of experience and professional discussions, as well as free seminars and master classes in provinces of Georgia for the young people who'd like to study graphic design or art but do not have such opportunity in their areas. All creatives, from experienced professionals to upcoming talents are welcome!
